Deep Look Into the Titanic Survivors

"The Titanic Analysis" presents a comprehensive and profound exploration of the survivors of the Titanic disaster. By meticulously dissecting passenger demographics, ticket classes, and available lifeboats, this study unveils nuanced insights into the factors that influenced survival rates. Through this detailed examination, a clearer understanding emerges of how socio-economic status, gender, and age played pivotal roles in the outcome of this historic tragedy.
